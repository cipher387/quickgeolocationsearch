Quick geolocation search

This simple tool helps save a lot of time when gathering information about geolocation. Enter latitude and longitude (or choose location on map) once and click the buttons with the names of online maps, to quickly jump to it.

So far there are only 15 sites collected here, but I plan to add more in the near future. Follow the news Follow @cyb_detective
